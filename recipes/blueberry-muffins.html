Dazzling Blueberry Muffins

Description

"Experience the magic of muffins with our Dazzling Blueberry Muffins. Bursting with juicy blueberries, these little wonders will add a splash of color and joy to your breakfast or snack time. Get ready for a dazzling culinary adventure!"

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  6. Spoon the batter into muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Let the muffins cool a bit, then get ready to be dazzled by the deliciousness!
