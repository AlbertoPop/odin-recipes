Sarmale

Description

"Delight in a Romanian culinary classic with our flavorful and expertly crafted sarmale. Tender cabbage leaves embrace a delicious filling of seasoned minced meat and rice, creating a savory symphony of taste. Elevate your dining experience with these delectable rolls, a true celebration of tradition and taste."

Ingredients

  • 1 fermented cabbage
  • 500g minced pork
  • 200g rice
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 500ml tomato broth
  • Oil for sautéing
  • 1 fermented cabbage

Steps

  1. Separate the cabbage leaves and boil them in salted water until slightly tender. Drain and cut away the thicker parts of the veins.
  2. auté the onion in oil until golden. Add the garlic and sauté for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the minced meat and brown it until it turns brown.
  4. Add the rice, tomato paste, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Place a cabbage leaf on a flat surface, add the meat mixture, and roll tightly.
  6. In a larger pot, arrange the rolled cabbage leaves. Add bay leaves and tomato broth.
  7. Let it simmer on low heat for about 1-2 hours or until the sarmale are tender, and the flavors meld.
  8. Serve with sour cream or hot peppers, according to taste.
